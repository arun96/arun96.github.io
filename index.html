Hi, I'm Arun Das.

I'm a scientist interested in the intersection of Computer Science and Genomics.

I recently completed my PhD in Computer Science under the supervision of Dr. Michael Schatz at Johns Hopkins University, on the topic of algorithms for improving accessibility and representation in large genomic studies.
